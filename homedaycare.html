Montessori Home Daycare

Home /Programs/Montessori Home Daycare

What we provide:

  • Long hours of Montessori care for your developing young ones (9 am to 5:30 pm)
  • We have a toddler program to help your child understand Montessori at an early age with credentialed Infant toddler teachers
  • Hot lunch prepared by our in house cook and served freshly made everyday. Kids also bake and do food preparation with our teachers. Snacks are also included.
  • Newsletter sent monthly to help the parents understand what we do inside the classroom
  • We have Brightwheel app to keep open communication with parents and teachers.
  • We help out the parents and their child adjust from home to school to big school.
